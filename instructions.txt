Tetris

Intro
 If you don't know how to play this game, may [gG]od
 have mercy on you--I sure won't. Contact me for 
 comments, features, and bugs. <tetris@angelforge.com>

Instructions
 - left, right, down to move
 - space to drop
 - ctrl, up to rotate
 - p to pause
 - enter, home to restart
